AVID, Advancement via individual determination is a program offered at Freehold High School for those students to are determined to do better in their classes. We give them the extra support needed to meet their goals.


Orangization goals

  1. All AVID seniors must attend college.
  2. All AVID students maintain a good average in all of their classes.
  3. All AVID students participate in class activities includingnote-taking strategies and tutorology.
  4. All AVID students are good role-models for other students.
